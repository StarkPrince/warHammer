What is incarnation?
 Incarnation literally means embodied in flesh or taking on flesh. It refers to the conception and birth of a sentient being who is the material manifestation of an entity, god, spiritual or universal force whose original nature is immaterial.
Why do we incarnate?
 Lord Shri Krishna has answered this in the Shrimad Bhagwat Gita When Arjuna asks Lord Shri Krishna why does God incarnate, then Lord Krishna answers this to Arjuna with a shloka. That verse is -
यदा यदा हि धर्मस्य ग्लानिर्भवति भारत ।
अभ्युत्थानमधर्मस्य तदात्मानं सृजाम्यहम् ॥७॥
परित्राणाय साधूनां विनाशाय च दुष्कृताम् ।
धर्मसंस्थापनार्थाय सम्भवामि युगे युगे ॥८॥
(श्रीमद्भगवद्गीता, अध्याय ४)
Spirituality: When religion starts to get lost and iniquity starts moving, then I create myself, that is, I am born. For the protection of the gentlemen and the destruction of the wicked and the restoration of religion, I descend into different eras.
